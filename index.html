    Border Collie

    ♠Click on my picture for more of me!

    These are very smart dogs. They are able to learn as much as you teach them. How could you not want a dog that will challenge you and is always active. However, if you aren't willing to give this dog a lot of attention then they can be a handful as they are very high energy.

    Beagle

    ♤Click on my picture for more of me!

    Now these dogs are very loud but in a good way. They are cute and cuddley. Who could say know no to this sweet puppy. They are great dogs for hunting. They have the different kinds of bark. They sometimes howl when they are bored, or when they are hunting, or trying to communicate with us humans.

    The Labrador

    ♝Click on my picture for more of me!

    I have had a black labrador retriever and these dogs are really loyal dogs. If you are looking for a family pet then look no further. This dog is great with kids and for learning new tricks. However, this breed generally can show signs of hip displacia and sometimes have anxiety about being left alone. If you were to consider this type of dog, make sure that someone will be with the dog, otherwise you might want to make your house/apartment dog proof.
